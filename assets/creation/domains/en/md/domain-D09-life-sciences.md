## 1. Purpose of the Investigation in This Domain

![Life Sciences: Creation Supported by Metabolism, Structure Recorded by the Body — 調査概要インフォグラフィック](https://uminomae.github.io/pjdhiro/assets/creation/img/svg/domains/ja/D09-01-overview-svg.svg)


This report presents the results of an investigation into whether structural correspondence with the five-stage model of creation, Field -> Wave -> Edge -> Vortex -> Bundle, can be found in the life sciences, including biochemistry, metabolism, cell biology, developmental biology, and immunology.

Where neuroscience (D08) treats the brain as information processing, the life sciences considered here deal with life as energy, metabolism, and material process. Retaining a question requires energy. Learning changes the brain's physical structure. Cells digest parts of themselves and reuse them. This document is an investigation report. It does not use findings from the life sciences to justify the theory.

## 2. Method of Investigation

In the life-science domain, eleven theories and phenomena were selected for evaluation. The selection criterion was that they be established biological processes in which structure is generated, maintained, or refined through transformations of energy, metabolism, and matter. The cases include metabolic foundations such as the astrocyte-neuron lactate shuttle, mitochondrial metabolism, and the glymphatic system; neurochemical processes such as dopamine reward-prediction error; developmental and refinement processes such as microglial pruning and myelination; stress response such as allostatic load; and basic life processes such as sexual reproduction, autophagy, morphogen gradients, and adaptive immunity.

For each theory, correspondence with the five-stage model was examined stage by stage, with particular attention to the three conditions associated with Edge: relational network, indeterminacy, and connection to Vortex.

## 3. Overview of the Five-Stage Model

This section explains the five-stage model used as the reference for the investigation. It is a model for describing the generation of existence itself, and is not limited to any particular scale or domain.

**Field**: An undifferentiated state. It is an initial condition in which neither direction nor structure has yet been fixed, a zero-state in which nothing has yet taken shape.

**Wave**: A stage of exploration in which multiple directions diverge and compete. Difference appears within what had been undifferentiated, and tension and fluctuation emerge.

**Edge**: A tense condition in which opposing elements coexist without converging into either side. This is the core stage of the model. The claim is that remaining at the boundary, or verge, is the essence of creation.

**Vortex**: A stage in which a new coherence or order rises spontaneously out of the tension. Contradictions retained at Edge are reorganized into a higher-order structure. In the life sciences, this appears as maintenance of long-term potentiation, plastic change, refinement through microglial phagocytosis, the emergence of a new diploid organism through fertilization, engulfment by isolation membranes in autophagy, and convergence through inter-clonal competition.

**Bundle**: A stage in which form is fixed and stabilized as a reusable structure. In the life sciences, this appears as long-term memory, myelin structure, offspring as a new genetic combination, fixed spatial pattern, and immune memory. Bundle is not final fixation; it becomes the precondition for a new Field.

If these definitions already produce discomfort, that is itself an important point for the investigation. One meaningful option is to continue reading while holding onto that discomfort. Another is to stop here. Both are legitimate choices.

## 4. Results of the Investigation

![Life Sciences: Creation Supported by Metabolism, Structure Recorded by the Body — 理論×5段階対応マトリクス](https://uminomae.github.io/pjdhiro/assets/creation/img/svg/domains/ja/D09-02-theories-map-svg.svg)


### Overall Picture

Eleven life-scientific structures were evaluated, and structural correspondence was confirmed in all eleven. At the same time, the character of the correspondence falls into three levels. The first six cases, metabolic foundations, provide the infrastructure of the five stages. The last four, basic life processes, are direct embodiments of the five stages at levels that do not involve consciousness. Dopamine reward-prediction error, D09-007, stands at the boundary with neuroscience (D08).

Nine of the eleven cases are life processes without consciousness. This strengthens further the finding from D08 that the five stages operate regardless of whether consciousness is present.

### Main Findings

#### Metabolic Foundations: Creation Has a Cost

The astrocyte-neuron lactate shuttle, the ANLS hypothesis, describes a structure in which different cell types are linked cyclically across a boundary and function through exchange of material. Retaining a question literally requires ATP, and astrocytes provide metabolic support. The hypothesis remains debated, but evidence has been reported that astrocyte-derived lactate contributes to memory formation under specific conditions.

The mitochondrial energy budget shows that because not all errors can be processed simultaneously, what can be taken up as a question is filtered through metabolic budget. That metabolic constraint is the physical background of error that would otherwise be rejected.

The glymphatic system is the brain's waste-clearance system that is activated during sleep. It is a reset process that clears metabolic byproducts accumulated during waking five-stage cycles and prepares the Field for the next cycle.

#### Dopamine: The Biochemical Instant of Lack

Midbrain dopamine neurons show phasic burst firing in response to unexpected reward and phasic dip activity when expected reward is omitted. This phasic dip is a biochemical record of the moment when what was expected did not arrive. It is the molecular-level counterpart of the structure in which discrepancy between prediction and reality is taken up as lack.

#### Destructive Creation: Pruning and Autophagy

Microglial synaptic pruning is a refinement process in which unnecessary synapses are physically removed through complement-mediated tagging, especially via C1q and C3. In the ordinary version of the five-stage model, Vortex is the stage in which a new coherence rises. In pruning, however, the Vortex dismantles an existing coherence. It is a destructive Vortex. Creation is not completed without refinement through removal.

Autophagy is the process by which a cell breaks down and reuses its own components under conditions of nutrient deprivation. The structure in which deficiency, here the lack of nutrient, triggers the dismantling and reuse of part of the self is a cell-level counterpart of the idea that reusable material can be found within what would otherwise be discarded.

#### Sexual Reproduction: A Five-Stage Cycle That Has Been Turning for Four Billion Years

Sexual reproduction is the theory showing one of the highest levels of correspondence across all five stages. Gene pool as Field, generation of diversity through meiosis as Wave, encounter of gametes and sexual selection as Edge, emergence of a new diploid organism through fertilization as Vortex, offspring as Bundle, and then the next generation's gene pool as a return to Field. This cycle has continued for more than four billion years without consciousness.

The twofold cost of sex, relative to asexual reproduction, is a biological example of the cost of remaining at Edge. The fact that evolution has paid an efficiency cost in order to preserve diversity, that is, creative possibility, offers an important clue about the relation between the cost of retention and its return.

#### Adaptive Immunity: A Fourth Implementation of Edge Through Molecular Recognition

Clonal selection in adaptive immunity corresponds to the five stages as follows: a naive diversity pool as Field, activation through antigenic stimulation as Wave, selection through the tripartite relation among antigen-presenting cells and T or B cells as Edge, convergence through selection and inter-clonal competition as Vortex, and immune memory as Bundle.

The tripartite relation between APC and T cells, MHC presentation plus TCR recognition plus co-stimulation, is a structure in which different entities meet at a boundary and the success or failure of response is directly controlled by the specificity of molecular recognition. This is a close match to the essential description of Edge. To the three lineages of Edge implementation identified in D08, time lag, phase synchrony, and social engagement, life science adds a fourth lineage: specificity of molecular recognition.

#### Morphogen Gradients: Five Stages Along a Spatial Axis

Morphogenesis through morphogen gradients unfolds the five stages along a spatial axis: uniform initial condition as Field, emergence of a concentration gradient as Wave, fate branching through threshold as Edge, sharpening of boundaries as Vortex, and fixed spatial pattern as Bundle. Unlike the usual version of the five stages, which is described as a temporal process, this is a realization in spatial pattern.

## 5. Correspondence with the Five Stages

### Field: Metabolic Margin and Diversity Pools

Metabolic homeostasis in astrocyte support, mitochondrial metabolic capacity, the naive immune repertoire, the gene pool, and the uniform early embryo all function as Field in the life sciences. They are material and energetic bases that are not yet fully differentiated.

### Wave: Error Signals and the Generation of Diversity

Increase in energy demand, prediction error in the form of phasic burst and phasic dip, the generation of genetic diversity through meiosis, antigenic stimulation, and the emergence of morphogen gradients all correspond to Wave. Wave is the driving force that introduces difference into Field.

### Edge: Relational Selection at Boundaries

Exchange of material between astrocytes and neurons, pruning selection through complement tagging, encounters between gametes, the tripartite APC-T cell relation, and fate branching by threshold are all forms of Edge. In the life sciences, Edge appears as a structure in which different elements meet at a boundary and direction is determined by the quality of the relation. Two cases showed especially strong correspondence.

### Vortex: Construction and Destructive Refinement

Maintenance of long-term potentiation, microglial phagocytosis as a destructive Vortex, fertilization, engulfment by isolation membranes, convergence through inter-clonal competition, and self-organization all correspond to Vortex. A distinctive contribution of this domain is that Vortex functions not only as inclusion, but also as destructive inclusion, taking in something by consuming and dismantling it.

### Bundle: Material Record and Circulation

Long-term memory, myelin structure, offspring, reuse of degradation products, spatial pattern, and immune memory all correspond to Bundle. In the life sciences, Bundle is materially recorded and often circulates into the next Field.

## 6. Insights from This Domain

### Creation Has a Cost

Retaining a question requires ATP, and metabolic reset through sleep is a condition for restoring capacity. The five-stage process is not free. Without metabolic margin, what arises is rejected immediately. The cost of retaining a question is not a metaphor; it is a physical fact.

### Creation Includes Destruction

Microglial pruning and autophagy remove, break down, and reuse what is unnecessary. They show that Vortex can function not only as inclusion but also as destructive inclusion, and that creation cannot be completed without refinement.

### The Five Stages Are Not Limited to Time

Morphogen gradients unfold the five stages along a spatial axis. The finding that the structure appears not only as a temporal process but also as a spatial pattern raises the level of generality claimed for the five stages.

### A Fourth Implementation of Edge

Clonal selection in immunity implements Edge through specificity of molecular recognition, adding a fourth lineage to the implementations identified in D08. This strengthens further the hypothesis that Edge is not a specific mechanism but a structural feature.

## 7. Unresolved Questions

- **How to place destructive Vortex**: In pruning and autophagy, decomposition runs in the opposite direction from the definition of Vortex as the rise of a new coherence. It remains unsettled whether this should be treated as the inverse direction of the same structure or as something qualitatively different.
- **Failure modes of the five stages**: Allostatic load may describe a failure mode in which Wave is not processed at Edge but simply accumulates as pathology. It remains theoretically unclear whether such failure modes lie inside the five stages or outside them.
- **The scope of spatial five-stage structure**: If morphogen gradients show the five stages along a spatial axis, in what kinds of dimension can the five stages hold? Whether they can be applied beyond time and space remains a task for future work.
- **The overwhelming absence of consciousness**: Nine of the eleven cases are life processes without consciousness. This reopens the question of how far any definition centered on consciousness can reach.

## 8. Summary

Life science is a domain that showed a high correspondence rate, with all eleven cases accepted. It provides the material basis of the five stages from the viewpoint of life as energy, metabolism, and matter.

The highest-confidence finding is the metabolic cost of creation. Retaining a question literally requires ATP, and restoring capacity requires metabolic reset through sleep. That is a settled physiological finding.

Other strong findings are that sexual reproduction has been running as a full five-stage cycle for more than four billion years, and that adaptive immunity provides a fourth implementation of Edge through specificity of molecular recognition.

More tentative findings concern destructive Vortex, in pruning and autophagy, and spatial five-stage structure, in morphogen gradients. Both extend the concept of the five stages and require further theoretical examination.

The unresolved question that remains most forceful is the overwhelming absence of consciousness. Life science is one of the domains that provides the largest body of evidence that the five stages operate without consciousness, and it repeatedly returns the question of what consciousness adds to creation.
