Cross-Domain Analysis of 30 Fields

Integrated Findings from Structural Similarity Exploration

This deck summarizes the common structure of the five-stage model found across 30 fields, from mathematics to traditional knowledge.

Phase 8 Five-Theme Integrated Structure Diagram

Stage-Level Distribution

Scale of Investigation

  • Fields covered: 30 academic fields (D01-D30)
  • Patterns extracted: 150 total (5 per field)
  • Cross-cutting themes: 5
  • Maximum convergence: 28/30 fields converged on a single theme
  • Position: Integrated summary of Phases 1-8

Distribution by Stage

  • En: 28 fields. The most frequent. Polymorphic concept family
  • Taba: 22 fields. Circulation and dynamism concentrated here
  • Ba: 15 fields. Multi-layered, conditioned potentiality
  • Uzu: 11 fields. Bidirectionality of creative/destructive emergence
  • Nami: 9 fields. The least theorized stage

Conclusion: En is the core; Nami remains the largest open territory.

Theme 1: Typology of En

  • Support: 28/30 fields
  • Claim: En is not one concept but a concept family
  • Representative types:

Mathematics: boundary condensation

Physics / Earth science: phase transition

Architecture: zone design

Performing arts: operational definition

Religious studies: passivity inversion

Core insight: En is not a static boundary but a dynamic process of simultaneous selection and generation.

Theme 2: Threshold Structure

  • Support: 11 fields
  • Claim: En-to-Uzu transition has discontinuous conditions
  • Representative forms:

Quantitative: physics, astronomy

Controllable: chemistry

Probabilistic: complexity science

Developmental: religious studies, developmental psychology

  • Key point: 10 fields describe "Uzu-unreached pathways"

Core insight: Not every En reaches Uzu.

Theme 3: Recirculation Mechanisms

  • Support: 19 fields
  • Claim: Taba is not an endpoint but the seed of the next Ba
  • Representative modes:

natural return

active return

dramatic return

unstable Taba

branching Taba

Core insight: Taba-to-Ba is not return to the same field, but an irreversible spiral.

Theme 4: Multi-layered Ba

  • Support: 15 fields
  • Claim: Ba is not "nothingness" but structured potentiality
  • Two types:

primordial Ba: structure does not yet exist

cyclical Ba: conditioned state produced by prior Taba

  • Representative fields: astronomy, earth science, evolutionary biology

Core insight: Ba is not a passive precondition but an active structure that shapes what follows.

Theme 5: Blind Spots and Counterexamples

  • Raised by: 13 fields
  • Blind spots:

treatment of order dissolution

resistance to staging

implicit assumptions: temporality, consciousness, energy supply

  • Representative case: mass extinction in earth science as destructive Uzu

Core insight: The five-stage model describes order generation, not every kind of change.

Five-Theme Structural Network

How the Five Themes Connect

  • Multi-layered Ba: defines the starting condition
  • Typology of En: describes the form of the contact zone
  • Threshold Structure: specifies conditions for En-to-Uzu transition
  • Recirculation Mechanisms: explains how Taba returns as the next Ba
  • Blind Spots / Counterexamples: mark limits and failure paths

Reading rule: The five themes are not separate topics but coordinated descriptions of Ba -> En -> Uzu -> Taba -> Ba.

Proposals for Stage Definitions

  • Ba: distinguish primordial Ba and cyclical Ba
  • En: expand from one definition to a concept family
  • Uzu: introduce bidirectionality of creative and destructive Uzu
  • Taba: redefine as the seed of spiral recirculation, not a terminal endpoint

Most robust update: the cyclical nature of Taba.

Implication: cross-domain comparison is advanced, but theoretical consolidation is still ongoing.

Scope of the Model

  • Can do: compare whether theories from different fields point to the same structure
  • Cannot do: explain every change or every process of dissolution
  • Strength: provides a shared coordinate system for cross-domain comparison
  • Weakness: Nami, failure modes, and non-Western perspectives remain underdeveloped

Position: not a total theory, but a structural lens for comparison.

Summary

  • Universality of En: 28/30 fields describe its polymorphism
  • Robustness of recirculation: 15+ fields support the Taba-to-Ba spiral
  • Redefinition of Ba: Ba is conditioned potentiality, not nothingness
  • Honest limits: blind spots and counterexamples are explicit
