## Conclusion

**The five stages should be redescribed not as a linear model but as a spiral model.**

The bundle never achieves complete stability; it contains within itself the seed of the next field. This "incompleteness of the bundle" is the driving force of circulation, a robust finding supported independently by 19 domains.

Systematization of the conditions for cessation of circulation remains an important open question.
