# T6: Field Multi-Layering — Conclusion Summary

15 of 30 domains directly thematize the multi-layered nature of "field." In stage-definition proposals, 28 of 30 reference "field."

- **The distinction between primordial field and circulatory field is universal**: Independently proposed by 10 domains. First-cycle and subsequent-cycle fields differ in nature
- **Field is not "emptiness" but "conditional potentiality"**: Quantum vacuum, determinability, and blank-space converge. "Zero, nothing" needs revision
- **Field operates as the foundational condition for all stages**: Both the "first stage" and the "ground of all stages"
- **The quality of the circulatory field determines the quality of creation**: Even the same "field" varies in quality, depending on how the previous bundle was formed

D13 philosophy and D24 religion propose three levels (experiential / ontological / ultimate), but the natural sciences suggest two categories suffice. Whether the third level falls within the scope of the five-stage model remains an open question. A structural tension exists: expanding the definition of field blurs the boundary with wave.
