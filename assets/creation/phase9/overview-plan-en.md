# Phase 9 — Deep Exploration Plan

![Phase 9 Deep Exploration Plan — Plan Overview Infographic](https://uminomae.github.io/pjdhiro/assets/creation/img/svg/phase9/ja/overview-01-overview-svg.svg)

## Context

Phase 8 identified five structural themes that recur across 30 academic domains. Phase 9 takes these findings deeper: How well do they hold up against existing scholarship? Where do they break down? Can they be extended to new domains? Can they be formalized mathematically?

## Four Tracks

| Track | Name | Purpose |
|-------|------|---------|
| 9A | Academic Grounding | Compare structural themes against existing scholarship to separate supported claims from untested ones |
| 9B | Falsification Search | Actively seek counterexamples and failure cases to eliminate confirmation bias |
| 9C | Frontier Domain Exploration | Apply the model to domains outside the original 30 to test predictive power |
| 9D | Mathematical Formalization | Express structural themes in mathematical notation to enable theorem derivation and prediction |

## Dependencies

```
9A Grounding ───→ 9B Falsification
     │                   │
     │                   ↓
     └────────→ 9D Formalization

9C Frontier (can start independently)
```

- **9A → 9B**: Falsification is most effective after grounding results are available
- **9A, 9B → 9D**: Formalization targets revised claims from 9A and 9B
- **9C**: Independent of other tracks, though results from 9A/9B improve prediction accuracy

## Recommended Start Order

1. **9A Academic Grounding** — Start first, beginning with Edge Typology (28 domains)
2. **9C Frontier Exploration** — Can run in parallel with 9A
3. **9B Falsification Search** — After 9A produces initial results
4. **9D Mathematical Formalization** — After 9A/9B revised claims are available

## Current Status

All four tracks are in the planning stage. Detailed investigation plans are available through each track's card.
